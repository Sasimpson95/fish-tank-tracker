FishTankTracker Account Deletion

Last updated: 3 July 2026

FishTankTracker allows users to request deletion of their account and associated app data. This page explains how to request account deletion and what data will be removed.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your FishTankTracker account, please email:

support.fishtanktracker@gmail.com

Please include the email address used to create your FishTankTracker account so we can identify and process your request.

What data will be deleted

When your account deletion request is processed, we will delete account data associated with your FishTankTracker account, including:

  • Your account email address
  • Your household and tank records
  • Water test records
  • Water change records
  • Livestock records
  • Maintenance records
  • Medication records
  • Feeding records
  • Reminder records
  • Uploaded aquarium photos, if applicable

Data retention

Account deletion requests are normally processed within 30 days. Some limited information may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or record keeping obligations.

Deleting some data without deleting your account

You may delete some aquarium records directly inside the FishTankTracker app where available. If you need help deleting specific data without deleting your account, contact us at:

support.fishtanktracker@gmail.com

Contact

If you have any questions about account deletion or data privacy, please contact:

support.fishtanktracker@gmail.com
