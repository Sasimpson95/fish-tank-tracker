Fish Tank Tracker Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1 July 2026

Fish Tank Tracker respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Email address (used for account sign in)
  • Aquarium information you choose to save
  • Water test records
  • Livestock records
  • Maintenance records
  • Reminder information

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the features of Fish Tank Tracker including:

  • Saving your aquarium data
  • Synchronising your data between devices
  • Providing reminders and health tracking
  • Improving app reliability

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase services.

Only you can access your aquarium data through your account.

Data Sharing

Fish Tank Tracker does not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and stored data at any time.

Children's Privacy

Fish Tank Tracker is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
